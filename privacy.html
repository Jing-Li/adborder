Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At AdBorder (adborder.top), your privacy is our top priority. We built our tool with a privacy-first architecture that ensures your images and personal data are never compromised.

Image Processing

All image processing on AdBorder happens entirely within your web browser using JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, never stored in any database, and never transmitted to any third party. When you close or refresh the page, all image data is completely gone.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal anonymous usage data through standard web analytics to understand how visitors use our tool and to improve the user experience. This data includes page views, browser type, approximate geographic location (country level), and referral source. We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses.

Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdSense to support the free operation of this tool. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings page at https://www.google.com/settings/ads.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of the website.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google Analytics (GA4) — for understanding site traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics collects anonymous data including page views, browser type, approximate geographic location (country level), session duration, and referral source. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense — for serving advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Your Rights

Regardless of your location, we respect the following rights regarding your personal data: the right to access any data we hold about you, the right to request deletion of your data, and the right to opt out of analytics tracking. Since AdBorder processes all images locally in your browser and collects no personal identifiers, there is typically no personal data to access or delete. For analytics data, you can opt out using the tools linked above. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lkong.top@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

AdBorder does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at lkong.top@gmail.com or visit our Contact page.
