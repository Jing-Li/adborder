About AdBorder

AdBorder is a free online tool designed to make adding borders to images as simple and fast as possible. Whether you need a white border for your Instagram posts, a polaroid-style frame for your photos, a gradient border for social media, or rounded corners for profile pictures, AdBorder handles it all in seconds.

Why We Built AdBorder

We noticed that most online image editing tools either require account creation, charge for basic features, add watermarks to your output, or upload your images to their servers. We believed that adding a simple border to an image should be free, instant, and private. So we built AdBorder.

Privacy-First Architecture

AdBorder is built with a client-side-only architecture. All image processing happens in your web browser using the HTML5 Canvas API and JavaScript. Your images never leave your device. No uploads, no server storage, no data collection. When you close the page, everything is gone. This is not just a feature — it is a fundamental design principle.

Free Forever

Every feature on AdBorder is completely free with no limits. No premium tiers, no usage caps, no watermarks on your output. We support the tool through non-intrusive advertising. We believe that useful tools should be accessible to everyone.

Built for Speed

AdBorder is a single-page application with no external frameworks or dependencies. It loads in under a second and processes images instantly. Our goal is to get you from upload to download in under 10 seconds.

If you have feedback, suggestions, or just want to say hello, you can reach us through our website. We appreciate all feedback and use it to improve AdBorder.
