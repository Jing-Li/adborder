How to Add a Border to an Image

Adding a border to your image takes just four simple steps:

Step 1: Go to adborder.top and drag and drop your image into the upload area, or click to browse. You can also paste from clipboard with Ctrl+V (Cmd+V on Mac).

Step 2: Choose a border style — Solid Color, Polaroid, Gradient, or Rounded Corners.

Step 3: Customize the border width with the slider, pick a color or gradient, and optionally add a drop shadow.

Step 4: Preview the result in real-time, choose your export format (PNG, JPEG, or WebP), and click Download.

That's it — no account needed, no software to install, and your image is processed entirely in your browser for complete privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a border to an image online for free? ▼
Go to adborder.top, drag and drop or paste your image, choose your border style (solid color, polaroid, gradient, or rounded), adjust the border width and color using the controls, preview the result in real-time, and click Download. How do I add a polaroid-style border to my photo? ▼

Yes, your images are completely safe. AdBorder processes all images locally in your web browser using JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API.
