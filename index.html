How to Add a Border to an Image

Adding a border to your image takes just four simple steps with AdBorder:

Step 1: Drag and drop your image, or click to browse. You can also paste from clipboard with Ctrl+V.

Step 2: Choose a border style — Solid Color, Polaroid, Gradient, or Rounded Corners.

Step 3: Customize width, color, or gradient, and optionally add a drop shadow.

Step 4: Preview in real-time, choose your export format, and click Download. That's it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a border to an image online for free?▼
Go to adborder.top, drag and drop or paste your image, choose your border style, adjust the width and color, preview in real-time, and click Download. No signup required, no watermark on output.
What is the best free tool to add a white border to photos?▼
AdBorder is one of the best free tools for adding white borders. It requires no account, adds no watermark, and processes images entirely in your browser for complete privacy. Supports widths from 1px to 200px.
Can I add a colored border without Photoshop?▼
Yes. Use the color picker to select any color, adjust the width with a slider, and download in PNG, JPEG, or WebP. You can also choose from gradient presets for creative styles.
How do I add a polaroid-style border?▼
Select the Polaroid preset to add a white border with a thicker bottom section that mimics classic Polaroid film. Add a drop shadow for a more realistic effect.
Is my image safe when using AdBorder?▼
Your images are completely safe. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript and Canvas API. Images are never uploaded to any server.
What image formats are supported?▼
Input: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, SVG. Output: PNG (lossless), JPEG (adjustable quality), or WebP (modern, excellent compression).
Can I add borders to multiple images at once?▼
Yes. Upload multiple images, apply the same settings to all, and download all results at once. Much faster than one by one.
How do I add a border for Instagram posts?▼
Use the IG Square preset for 1:1 ratio with a white border, or IG Story for 9:16. Your photos display perfectly on Instagram without unwanted cropping.
