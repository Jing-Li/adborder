Frequently Asked Questions

Is AdBorder free?

Yes, AdBorder is completely free with no limits, no watermarks, and no premium tiers.

Do I need to create an account?

No. AdBorder works instantly in your browser with no signup required.

Are my images safe?

Absolutely. All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server.

What browsers are supported?

AdBorder works in all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Mobile browsers are also supported.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all emails within 48 hours. For urgent issues, please include "Urgent" in your subject line.
